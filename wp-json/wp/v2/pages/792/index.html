World Medical Innovation Forum
\n<\/strong>Cancer<\/h2>\n

Boston, Massachusetts
\nApril 25-27, 2016
\nWMIF@partners.org<\/b><\/a><\/p>\n

The World Medical Innovation Forum is a global gathering of more than 1,100 senior health care leaders hosted by Partners HealthCare in the heart of Boston. It was established to respond to the intensifying transformation of health care and its impact on innovation. The Forum is rooted in the belief that no matter the magnitude of that change, the center of health care needs to be a shared, fundamental commitment to collaborative innovation \u2013 industry and academia working together and its ability to improve patient lives.<\/p>\n

Selected industry leading organizations committed to innovation and its ability to improve care sponsor the Forum. Supporting the Forum brings visibility and insight to strategic decision-making on the most important industry and cross-industry related issues, and provides visibility in an environment ideally suited to engaging with the most senior leaders in global health care and medical innovation.<\/p>\n

The 2016 World Medical Innovation Forum will highlight state-of-the-art and emerging approaches to diagnosing, treating and managing cancer\u2014including early cancer detection and prevention; precision medicine and evolving models of care; immunological therapies; combination therapies to circumvent resistance; methods to find and develop new targets and new drugs as well as new informatics-driven tools and research approaches.<\/p>\n

Experts present the most compelling advances in technology, care, research and unmet needs in a key area of care\u2014the 2016 focus is cancer. Confirmed speakers include CEOs and top executives of dozens of global medical companies\u2014such as Joseph Jimenez, CEO, Novartis and Robert Mulroy, CEO, Merrimack Pharmaceuticals\u2014investors, media leaders, and internationally recognized Partners experts\u2014Harvard Medical School faculty\u2014including Daniel Haber, MD, PhD and Monica Bertagnolli, MD. In addition to examining the cutting-edge issues in the field, the Forum will feature the \u201cDisruptive Dozen\u201d\u201412 emerging technologies with the potential to revolutionize the selected field over the next decade.<\/p>\n\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t<\/div>\n<\/div><\/div><\/div>
